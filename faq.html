FAQs

What are the Course objectives?

At the end of this course the candidate will be able to:

  • Describe the pathophysiology, presentation, diagnosis of diabetes and its complications
  • Evaluate the management options for adults newly diagnosed with diabetes (Type 1 or 2, gestational, or secondary diabetes)
  • Understand the importance of lifestyle modifications in the prevention of diabetes and its complications
  • Understand the effective management of obesity
  • Apply national and international guidelines and evidence to the practice of diabetes care in India
  • Understand the use of oral anti diabetic drugs, insulin and other management options for diabetes

What is the Course Duration?

  • This is an online certificate course of six months duration. In case candidate is not able to complete the course as per schedule, the extension of the course duration will be on BMJ discretion.

What is the Course Structure?

It is a six month online modular course with six modular units and 1 contact program during the tenure of the course, The contact program will be held at Fortis C-DOC , Nehru Place, New Delhi.

  • Six online self learning units
  • Clinical rotation across all diabetes speciality (eg foot care, ICU, etc)
  • Clinical evaluation by examiners at the end of contact Program

What are the Eligibility Criteria?

MBBS graduates or post graduates in any speciality registered with Medical Council of India(MCI) or any of the state medical councils.

Is it affiliated or recognized by MCI or any other bodies?

It is not yet affiliated or recognised by MCI or any other medical body. The successful candidates will be jointly awarded a certificate by BMJ, Fortis C-DOC and The Royal College of Physicians.

What all will be covered in contact programs?

This involves class-room teaching and hands-on training on the various aspects of diabetes. This program will be conducted four times a year in batches of 8-10 student doctors per program. The sessions will be from 9 am to 6 pm and all practical aspects of diabetes will be covered. The candidate will get extensive exposure to various specialised clinics (obesity and weight loss, male hormonal clinic) OPD patients , case work-up, ophthalmology clinic for fundus examination , foot care clinic , diet clinic.

What will be the duration of contact program?

Duration of the contact program is three days.

The Schedule for the contact programs:

  • 1. Introduction to the Course, meeting the faculty.
  • 2. Clinical rotation across all diabetes speciality (eg foot care, etc)
  • 3. Clinical hands on training
    Evaluation (MCQs, OSCE followed by viva)

Where these contact programs will be held?

The contact programs will be held at Fortis C-DOC Center, Chirag Enclave, New Delhi

How I can register myself for the course?

Interested candidates can download the APPLICATION FORM from our website and send us the filled application form with required details along with course fee.

What are the criteria for successfully completing the course?

To be awarded the Certificate in Diabetes the candidate must complete all of the following within 6 months of starting the programme:

  • Attend the hands on training/clinical observership course at Fortis C-DOC and behave in an acceptable and professional manner as assessed by the supervising chief of staff
  • Be judged by the examiners on a clinical evaluation day at Fortis C-DOC to meet (at least) the following statement "This is a doctor with a basic understanding of diabetes care who we would expect to practice professionally and within their capabilities in their particular area of clinical responsibility."
